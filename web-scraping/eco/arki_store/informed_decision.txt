1. High Price Bar (Premium Products)
English:
- Attract Customers:
  - Offer exclusive deals like limited editions or early access to special collections. This makes the product feel unique and worth the higher price.
- Stay Competitive:
  - Provide excellent customer service (fast responses, easy returns) to justify the premium price.
  - Regularly release new and innovative products to keep customers interested and excited.
